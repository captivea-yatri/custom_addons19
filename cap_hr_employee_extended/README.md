Cap Hr Employee Extended aims to simplify processes for enhanced easy to use.
=============================================================================

With just a click within the Cap HR Employee Extended, employees can access the Employee App and open the
Employee Form Views seamlessly.

About Cap Hr Employee Extended
==============================

This module is built upon hr.employee and hr.employee.public. Within hr.employee, all access including display
buttons and editable fields are available. you can access any button and fields into hr employee However, in 
hr.employee.public, no buttons are displayed and fewer fields are editable compared to hr.employee.

About Cap Hr Employee Extended Work follow
==========================================

In the Cap HR Employee Extended module, the Functional Knowledge Score and Global Knowledge Score fields are present.
The Functional Knowledge Score represents the calculated functional score, while the Global Knowledge Score represents
the calculated global knowledge score. Both fields, along with all buttons, have been set to readonly.
Here Cap Hr Employee Extended have Functional Knowledge Score and Global Knowledge Score Fields.
Functional Knowledge Score are calculated functional Score and Global Knowledge Score Calculated Global Knowledge Score.
Make all fields and button readonly.
