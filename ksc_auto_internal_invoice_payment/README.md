================================================
Odoo 19 Auto Internal Invoice Payment
================================================

This Module Extends Odoo's Accounting module functionality by enabling option to set different but unique combination of 'customer','company' and 'journal', for which your company needs payment to be done automatically


============
Odoo Version
============
Odoo 19.0


============
Dependencies
============
Following Modules must be already installed to have Cap Marketing CRM Automation functionality
1) Accounting
2) Product



=====================
Detailed Information
=====================
Follow steps to avail functionality:
To explore this functionality, make sure that you are in multi company environment and all companies are selected while making multi company transaction
(1) Go to Accounting>Configuration>Auto Internal Transaction Configuration and click on New button to create Combination
(2) Create and confirm Invoice or Bill with selecting combination of company and customer that you have configured in step 1
(3) On confirmation, status of Invoice or Bill will be automatically converted to 'In Payment'
