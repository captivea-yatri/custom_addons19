================================================
Odoo 19 Account Inter Company Rule By Captivea
================================================

This Module Improves the feature of default odoo accounting inter company rule


============
Odoo Version
============
Odoo 19.0


============
Dependencies
============
Following Modules must be already installed to have Cap Marketing CRM Automation functionality
1) Accounting
2) Purchase



=====================
Detailed Information
=====================
Follow steps to avail functionality:
To explore this functionality, make sure that you are in multi company environment and all companies are selected while making multi company transaction
(1) Go to Accounting>Customer>Invoices and click on New button to create new invoice
(2) Select the internal company that you need to make transaction to as customer and click on save
(3) after completing step 2, you can see that a draft bill is been created based on customer data in corresponding company which can be seen from field "Related Journal Entry"
(4) All the changes you make to the draft invoice will be reflected in draft bill automatically
(5) also, when you confirm the invoice, corresponding bill will also be confirmed
(6) when you convert confirmed invoice to draft by clicking on button "Reset to Draft", corresponding bill will also gets converted into Draft Automatically
